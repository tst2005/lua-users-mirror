Sorry about that. See WikiBugs for more on this issue. The problem doesn't appear on the stock Usemod since it doesn't have a separate preview page feature. I consider it a feature because of two points: 1) it saves 50% of data transfer compared to an in-line preview page which duplicates the text in the edit window and preview area (can make a difference for large pages over slow modems) and 2) it's less likely that you'll forget you are looking at a preview (that is, haven't commited your change) since the page has a unique background. -JohnBelmonte
