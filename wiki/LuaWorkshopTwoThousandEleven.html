Lua Workshop Two Thousand Eleven
wiki

Ongoing arrangements for the Lua Workshop 2011.

If you plan to participate in the workshop, register now at the official workshop page http://www.lua.org/wshop11.html

Date:

Thursday, September 8 and Friday, September 9. Arrival Wednesday, September 7. Departure Saturday September 10.

Venue:

  • FIBL, Frick, Switzerland

Located on a nice hillside between the two Swiss towns Basel and Zurich
in Switzerland is the FIBL, the Research Institute of Organic Farming
(see http://www.fibl.org/en/switzerland.html). They provide excellent
conference facilities in a nice and calm setting, which is inspiring to
the mind:
http://www.fibl.org/en/switzerland/location-ch/conference-centre.html

After a day of conference work, a wine tasting from their own
organically grown wines can round up a nice day and give opportunity for
talks and chatter.
http://www.fibl.org/en/switzerland/location-ch/winery.html

The FIBL is about 40mins by train from either Basel (airport code BSL)
or Zurich (airport code ZRH), located near the small town, or rather
village, of Frick, which has Hotels, Restaurants etc

[@Google Maps]

(very) preliminary Schedule

  • wednesday afternoon/evening: people arrive, maybe a small warm-up meeting somewhere
  • thursday: conference
  • thursday evening: wine tasting / "social event" (i.e. dinner after the tasting)
  • friday: conference
  • friday evening: ad-hoc organized dinner style gathering
  • saturday morning: people leave (or go visit other parts of switzerland, we can help you with travel ideas/plans/arrangements)

Transfers from the FIBL to the village centre/train station will be organized.

Day structure

  • 9:00 morning session starts
  • 10:30 coffe break
  • 11:00 - 13:00 hard work
  • 13:00 lunch
  • 14:00 afternoon session starts
  • 15:30 coffe break
  • 16:00 - 17:30 another round of hard work
