I'm a fairly small-time user of Lua. I've been attracted to it before because of its simplicity and its Palm implementation; this time I'm attracted to it because of its small size and number of dynamic library dependencies. I've used it most recently to do some simple CGI stuff with a chrooted webserver under OpenBSD. The product of this is cgi.lua, a bit of lua to get the variables from a form and let you write scripts to do things with them. You can see it, and me, at my homepage: http://j.agrue.info/
