Lua Workshop 2005

Breaking News: The workshop will be at Adobe [3]. Please register!

We're trying to determine whether there is enough interest in a Lua workshop.

If you're interested, please add your name to the list at the bottom of the page.

Possible Activities

  • Tutorial on Lua
    • the C API
    • binding C libraries
  • Plenary talk -- The evolution of Lua
  • Roundtable
    • The future of Lua
    • Lua in games
    • Lua in industry
    • Lua in embedded projects
    • Ready-made Lua installations
  • Track
    • Lua internals
    • Lua applications
  • Contributed talks -- Your experience using Lua

Possible Venues

  • Mark Hamburg at Adobe in San Jose, CA.
  • Erik Hougaard at E Foqus Denmark A/S in Copenhagen, Denmark.
  • Rio (the home of Lua!).

Possible Timing

  • How about around the time of the Game Developers Conference [4]? (that's early March 2005, which may be too early)
  • There is also OSS 2005 [5] in July.
