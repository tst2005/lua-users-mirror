Universally Unique Identifiers
wiki

Here are various ways to generate universally unique identifiers (UUID's) [1] in Lua.

  • Linux distributions may provide the 'uuid' command: local fh = assert(io.popen'uuid'); print(fh:read'*l'); fh:close() . This is also mentioned in [PiL].
  • [lhf's uuid] is a small C wrapper around libuuid
  • Lua/APR provides a [uuid_get] functions, wrapping the corresponding APR C function.
